Filed Criminal Complaint Summary

Defendant: Peggy (Alias: "Pegz", "VideoShaman88")

Filing Date: May 24, 2025

Case Number: NY-MH-5549382-24

Filed By: DA Violation Portal, Manhattan Division

Count 1: Digital Harassment of Authority Figures (Felony Class D)

The defendant repeatedly refused to acknowledge or respect the authority of chatroom moderators, specifically targeting user "TinkyWiinky" in the Jackielee100 video chatroom with inflammatory messages and undermining administrative decisions.

Count 2: Unsolicited Multimedia Dissemination (Misdemeanor)

The defendant spammed chatrooms with URLs linking to unsolicited homemade video content, disrupting communal interaction and violating platform usage policies.

Count 3: Celebrity Fraud by Association (Felony Class E)

The defendant spent extensive funds—reported to exceed $100,000—hiring C- and D-list actors to produce cameo videos falsely implying celebrity endorsement of her activities.

Count 4: False Identity Attribution (Felony Class C)

The defendant knowingly and falsely accused chatter "Lagbot" of being someone he is not, initiating a cascade of public misidentification and defamation.

Count 5: Digital Platform Poaching (Misdemeanor)

The defendant encouraged users to abandon a compliant chatroom ecosystem in favor of an unregulated one, fostering environments for increased rule-breaking.

Count 6: Post-Mortem Defamation (Felony Class D)

The defendant circulated false claims that deceased community member "Rodney" was still alive, resulting in public confusion and emotional distress among other chat participants.

Count 7: Cultural Misrepresentation Through Cosplay (Misdemeanor)

The defendant obsessively participated in culturally insensitive cosplay, misrepresenting and mocking various cultures in a manner deemed socially irresponsible.

Count 8: Digital Littering (Infraction)

Numerous blogs were created by the defendant without garnering a single visitor, contributing to server clutter and unnecessary internet bloat.

Count 9: Linguistic Coercion (Misdemeanor)

The defendant enforced the usage of grammatically incorrect language and spelling within group chats under false educational pretenses.

Count 10: Unauthorized Herbological Literacy Promotion (Infraction)

The defendant promoted the act of sprinkling basil on books as a valid method for reading comprehension, misleading vulnerable users in literacy development programs.

Total Charges Filed: 10 (4 Felonies, 4 Misdemeanors, 2 Infractions)
