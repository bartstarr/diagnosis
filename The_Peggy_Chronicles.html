👜 New York State of Mind

A vivid character study of Peggy Benda, the abrasive and insult-loving octogenarian whose rent-controlled kingdom doubles as her personal insult war room. A sarcastic deep-dive into luxury, loneliness, and lashing out.

📜 Teaser – Amor, frustración y conflicto: días oscuros, sin duda

A sultry scroll-text teaser of the novella’s themes—lost love, misplaced rage, psychiatric leaks, and virtual vendettas. Told with the sharpness of a Don Rickles zinger and the flair of vintage leather.

📖 A Disturbing Novella

The full novella that exposes Peggy’s tumultuous online escapades, complex virtual affairs, and her eventual unraveling. A dramatic, bizarre, and often hilarious descent into insult-fueled madness.

Catalog compiled by the brave scholar who once corrected Peggy on the difference between libel and slander—sparking a tantrum so loud, the neighbors thought she fell again. Victory never sounded so shrill.
