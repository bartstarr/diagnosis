Amor, frustración y conflicto: días oscuros, sin duda

A tale of one woman, many handbags, and the virtual battlegrounds that shaped her unraveling. Follow Peggy Benda, an aging social combatant armed with sarcasm and unresolved affection, as she navigates a digital wasteland of failed romances, hostile chatrooms, and emotionally constipated men.

From luxury purses to psychiatric leaks, and allies-turned-enemies-turned-allies again, this is a deliciously twisted dive into delusion, desperation, and a deeply misunderstood pension plan.
